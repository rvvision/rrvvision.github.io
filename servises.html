NYCI

The National Youth Council of India has offered and is continuing to offer it's sercices for multiple areas of society. Our leaders are placed all over India helping fellow citizens to gear up and fly

EDUCATION

NYCI has conducted educational workshops, online and offline, on multiple fields of education. These workshops have achieved great success in terms of participation and sharing knowledge. They also focus on establishing adequate and valid linkages between education and skill development, employment and entrepreneurship opportunities, cultural development, sport and recreation activities.

EVENTS

NYCI has conducted over fifty workshops for participants in and out of India. We have extended our webinars for students, teachers and people from everyother profession, all around the globe. Youth dialogues have impacted the younger population and given them great insight on their future. We also focus and encourage youth participation in the nation's holistic development. Empowerment of youth by educating them about diversity, culture, mianstream issues and sustainable development have been established.

PROMOTION

Our partnership programs benefit the company in terms of CSR projects as well as has a direct impact on our society. Support the local youth, who are the pillars of the nation, by partnering with us and promoting our events. Our partners include IRCTC, CYPAN, CIYF, Tamil Nadu Government, Times Now and many more esteemed organizations and institutions

One such endeavor is our attempting for the Asian book of records by conducting a 2-hour virtual yoga workshop. Since times immemorial, Yoga has not only been a form of exercise for the body and mind, but also the way of life for many. To promote this art of life itself, we have over 25,000 people from all over India who will be in attendance to observe and practice along with Asia's largest group of Yoga enthusiasts. The attempt will also include various yoga instructors who will guide the attendees of the event on different yoga asanas via a virtual platform. The event will begin at 8:30am with a welcome speech. From 9 to 9:10am, participants will hold two asanas for 5 minutes each, posing with vigour and strength, to symbolise our determination to spread the message of Yoga all over the world. 9:10 onwards, the main part of the event will kick off with many different and elaborate yoga asanas.

Our sponsor is SRM Institute of Science and Technology, a revered institute in India, aimed at empowering and engaging youth in the field of science and is one of India's finest and most well known academic institution. We are also sponsored by Brittania Industries, a legendary company that invigorates the youth of our nation with all of its wonderful products that fall under the category of food. Additionally, we are sponsored by Franch Express Courier, one of the leading courier services in South India and a pioneer at it's field if work.

As we continue to grow as an organization, we hold our partners with the highest regard and respect our partnerships with utmost loyalty as it is partnership that aids our organization in succeeding and progressing. Your support will not only help us reach our goal, but it will also help us aim higher each time we commence a project. Thank you for being an excellent example to our organization and for inspiring us to work even harder to make a difference.
