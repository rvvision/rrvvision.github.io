V - VISION

Tapping the latent abilities of the youth generation is indeed a great asset to both the country and to the people around those candidates. But the magnitude of work and the route map these youngsters have to take up in order to expose such prodigic abilities have been age long questions. V Vision aims at encountering these talents and exposing them to the world. V Vision is an Education Startup that helps students discover and pursue their Passions through large city wide Youth fests, summits and bootcamps. Founded in 2019, V vision is a year old company that aims at making a difference in the field of education. It is a platform that helps the young generation take the first step in their field of passion with the help of expert mentors, achievers and guides. The entity believes that learning starts from one’s childhood, thus it connects with School and College students to young achievers in their field of passion by tapping the potential out of the young buds. The students also join a community of like minded individuals from all across the country, attend V Vision meetups, events, internships and get workshop perks. While the tasks of V Vision do not end here. V Vision is proud to have been acclaimed as one of the best education based event management start up in Chennai. It has held hands with more than 150 colleges in India; with a huge network of resources, the company has drawn the attention of students across the country. It has conducted numerous talent events, where youngsters had to expose their latent abilities, thus enabling the world to know about these underrated talents.

V Vision is also proud to claim one of its events having been done with the National Unification Council of South Korea. Over the past 3 months, V Vision has organized 2 large-scale events. The former is the Youth Talent Icon where youth all across India showcased their talents, while the latter is South India’s first ever and largest virtual aero modelling workshop. The workshop was conducted for a period of 1.5 months and consisted of over 400 participants. The workshop ended with an interactive session with Dr. T. K. Sundaramurthy, Mission Director, ISRO (Retd.). V Vision’s next ambitious target is to spread mental awareness. With the constant pressure and complexions the youth are facing these days, V Vision wants the youth to speak for themselves. For this, an informative workshop on Applied Psychology will be conducted. It focuses on educating the participants on therapy, social psychology, personality psychology, optimism and non-verbal communication. It invites a panel of articulate speakers who are expertise on these subjects. Dr. Kannan Gireesh, Child Psychiatrist and founder of Live Life foundation; Dr Mini Rao, Psychologist; Ms. Kavitha Dhanraj, Psychology Professor and Ms. Priya Magesh, a dignified psychiatric counselor and psychotherapist are among the panelists. The workshop is expected to take place for a month, and the youngsters who participate will be awarded with acknowledgement certificates.

V Vision also hosts many educational and cultural activities, which speaks about the importance of youth, tradition, culture, global trends and mental health. It does articulate promotional work over the internet, and has connections across the globe. V Vision is a world record attempted company, whose sole purpose is to drive the youth towards their betterment.
